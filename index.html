Support

YNAB connection

Open MoneyHoney Settings to connect, reconnect, or disconnect YNAB. Access is read-only, and MoneyHoney cannot change your YNAB plan.

App access

If Face ID or device authentication does not complete, verify MoneyHoney has permission in iOS Settings and that your device passcode is enabled.

Notifications

Notification preferences are available in MoneyHoney Settings and iOS Settings. Lock-screen notification text is intentionally generic.

Delete local data

Disconnect YNAB to remove the access token. Deleting the app removes local app data; private iCloud data is managed through your Apple account settings.

Protect your financial information. Never include account balances, transaction details, passwords, or access tokens in a support request.

For help that is not covered above, open a support request. You can also review existing requests in the public support tracker.
